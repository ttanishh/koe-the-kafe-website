TASTE THE CRAFTMANSHIP

Original Coffee

Coffee roasting is the process of heating green coffee beans to develop flavor, aroma, and color. It involves carefully controlling temperature and time to achieve desired roast levels, ranging from light to dark. The roasted beans undergo chemical changes, producing the distinctive taste and fragrance associated with different coffee varieties. .

Self Roasted

During roasting, beans go through various stages: drying, first crack (audible when beans expand), and, if desired, a second crack. Roasters use different methods like air roasting or drum roasting, each influencing the final flavor profile. Factors such as bean origin, altitude, and processing also impact the roasting outcome, resulting in a diverse spectrum of coffee flavors appreciated by enthusiasts.
